Who Are We?
The Dalton Friends of the Wayne County Public Library is a non-profit organization founded by local volunteers and dedicated to promoting and supporting the services our library provides to the community.

The Friends provide funds for special purchases outside the library's publicly funded budget. Among the many purchases the Friends have made since the group was founded in 2007 are:

  • A portable sound system for outdoor programs
  • Replacement and additional toys for the children's area
  • Benches for the front porch
  • Seasonal indoor decorations
  • Gift baskets for library reading promotions
  • A refrigerator and microwave for the staff work area
  • Summer flowers in large pots at the library entrance
  • $10,000 toward library expansion in 2015
  • Sponsorship of selected library programs

Friends also provide hands-on help and refreshments at special events. Ongoing sale of shred bags at the library desk, annual memberships, and donations are our primary means of generating money. We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
