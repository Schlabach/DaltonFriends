Who are the Friends of the Library?

The Dalton Friends of the WCPL is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to promoting and supporting the services that our library provides to the community. The Dalton Friends of the Library members are passionate library advocates who love the library!

Follow Us on Facebook!

What do the Friends do?

The Friends of the Library help bring valuable programs and services to the community. Membership dues and other fundraising efforts go directly to supporting the Dalton Library by increasing financial resources beyond public funding. Among the many purchases the Friends have made since the group was founded in 2007 are:

  • A portable sound system for outdoor programs
  • Replacement and additional toys for the children's area
  • Benches for the front porch
  • Seasonal indoor decorations
  • Gift baskets for library reading promotions
  • A refrigerator and microwave for the staff work area
  • Summer flowers in large pots at the library entrance
  • $10,000 toward library expansion in 2015
  • Sponsorship of selected library programs

Meetings are held 2-3 times per year and all members are welcome but not obligated to attend. At meetings you’ll have the opportunity to network with other library supporters, share ideas and use your skills by volunteering in Friends activities. If you are not able to attend meetings and be an active member, that’s ok! Your membership is still valuable in providing support to the library. All donations to the Friends are recognized via a listing of contributors in the library building.

How can I become a Friend of the Dalton Library?

Join us in supporting our library - become a member or renew your membership today. Your donation to the 501 (c)(3) organization is tax-deductible to the extent allowable by law.
